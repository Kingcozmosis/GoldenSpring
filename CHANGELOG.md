# Changelog

All notable changes to GoldenSpring will be documented here.

## [1.0.0] — 2026-04-26

### Initial release
- Physics-based Rayleigh + Mie atmospheric scattering
- PCSS soft shadows with penumbra width based on blocker distance
- Coloured shadows from translucent blocks (stained glass, leaves)
- RSM global illumination with temporal denoising
- GTAO ambient occlusion
- 3D volumetric clouds with self-shadowing
- Volumetric fog and screen-space god rays
- Screen-space reflections (SSR) on water and smooth surfaces
- Wave parallax and animated water normals
- Underwater caustics and volumetric lighting
- ACES filmic tone mapping
- Temporal Anti-Aliasing (TAA)
- AMD FSR RCAS sharpening
- Bokeh depth of field
- Cinematic motion blur
- Dual-filter bloom
- Auto exposure
- Signature golden-hour colour grade
- Four performance profiles: Low / Medium / High / Ultra
- Full Nether and End world support
- Distant Horizons (DH) compatibility
